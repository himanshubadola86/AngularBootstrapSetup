AABHAR , is an NGO (society) registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860. Its main objectives are livelihood generation/enhancement, education/skill development, health/sanitation, drinking water, environment and cultural/historical/spiritual heritage protection/promotion with focus on rural/socio-economically backward areas.

OUR PARTNERS

We forge long-term partnerships with our partners who offer complementary approaches and are able to effectively contribute to our programs addressing the underlying causes. Our approach to partnership is to build shared and deeper understanding on issues that affect people’s wellbeing and focuses on capacity development of communities and local organisations.

ACCOUNTABILITY & TRANSPARENCY


ACCOUNTABILITY

Meeting international standards?

AABHAR India is committed to meeting international standards of quality and accountability. We focus on ensuring that our partners and the communities we work with have a voice in planning, implementing and evaluating our work. The progress of AABHAR Programmes/projects is/will be monitored and the organisational data is/will be published in our Annual Report

TRANSPARENCY

ANNUAL REPORTS AND PROJECT INFORMATION ?

Being accountable to our stakeholder’s means ensuring that we share information in a transparent way or, where we are unable to satisfy an information request, providing an explanation. AABHAR posts/will post adequate amount of information on the internet, including annual reports, programme/project reports, research reports, external evaluations, and media releases. We will also define the types of information that must remain confidential due to practical legal, operational, safety and security considerations. All stakeholders have the right to provide feedback to AABHAR, including a complaint, and to receive a response.

Zero Tolerance

Create safe work enviroment ?

Reporting Harassment, Sexual Exploitation and Abuse: We know people are sometimes reluctant to report sexual abuse or harassment. So we are focused and committed to creating a working environment at AABHAR where people feel safe to come forward with sexual harassment, exploitation or abuse claims. We take/will take reports extremely seriously and protect witnesses as well as victims.

FINANCIALS

AABHAR ensures that the contributions received are utilised for the benefit of rural poor and marginalised communities across our work, encompassing education, livelihood, and health and for protection of environment as well as heritage.
