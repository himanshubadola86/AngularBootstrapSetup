a little about us.

AABHAR is an NGO (society) registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860. Its main objectives are livelihood generation/enhancement, education/skill development, health/sanitation, drinking water, environment and cultural/historical/spiritual heritage protection/promotion with focus on rural/socio-economically backward areas. It does/will do so through well-planned and comprehensive programmes/projects in the mentioned focus areas in order that the people in the rural areas also live with dignity and security and avoid migrating to urban areas. The NGO operates with all India jurisdiction. The registered office of the society is in the National Capital region of Delhi
