Our Work



AABHAR , focuses on developing the potential of rural masses to drive long lasting equitable changes. We strategically work to help develop sustainable livelihood opportunities, promote quality healthcare, inclusive education, and protect environment and heritage. Our efforts are focused to fight against underlying causes of migration from rural areas and help ensure a life of dignity and security to the people of rural areas.

Our key program approaches include understanding underlying issues in respect of the focus areas and taking action in line with the defined objectives.

Livelihood

To select socio-economically backward area/areas and investigate means industrial, agricultural, educational or otherwise to generate income sources for the people of that area by implementing livelihood enhancement projects with the help of governmental or non-governmental agencies.

Education

To participate in planning and delivery of various schemes related to social welfare including health and to coordinate therefore with associated Government/ Semi Government/ Non-Government departments/agencies and the people of the local area
